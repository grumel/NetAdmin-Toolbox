# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in NetAdmin Toolbox welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Explain technical disagreements with evidence and curiosity.
- Welcome different perspectives and first-time contributors.
- Give credit and assume good intent.
- Protect private information and credentials.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, intimidation, unwanted contact, publishing private information, and other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately through the repository's GitHub contact options rather than opening a public issue. If private reporting is unavailable, contact the maintainer through the GitHub profile and do not include confidential details in a public thread. Reports will be reviewed fairly and handled confidentially where possible.

This code of conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
